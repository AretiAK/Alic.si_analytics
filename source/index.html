See the impact of your work,
in your data.

For any impact driven project, Alice analyses, visualises and matches your data with the impact measurement framework of your choice.

Try it free Follow our journey
    1

    Set your impact goals

    Create your project and identify your impact goals. Each goal is matched with the data upload and creates a dashboard of insights.

    2

    Upload your data

    If you gather your data through surveys or IoT devices, you can upload an excel or connect to your Salesforce, or other, database.

    3

    Match your framework

    Select an impact measurement framework (SDG, SROI, ESG, IRIS) or create your own. Your data will be compared to the framework targets and show on your dashboard.

Safe homes and financial support,
led by beneficiary data

Resonance, the UK’s leading property impact investment fund, houses over 1,600 financially vulnerable people in the UK.

Alice won a UK government grant with Resonance to measure the impact of their housing offering, through beneficiary-generated data.

Quality education for refugee
students, with tracked progress

Vodafone Foundation & UNHCR give young refugees access to digital learning content and the internet, offering quality education in some of the most marginalised areas in Africa.

Alice automates the analysis and visualisation of the programme’s impact data, enabling Vodafone Foundation to showcase the invaluable impact of their work.
